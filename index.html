Kashmiri Kahwa
Crafted in Kashmir
Born in the quiet mountain air of Kashmir, KAHWA HAVEN is composed as a slow ritual rather than a beverage.
Delicate saffron threads unfold beside rose petals, cardamom, green tea, and warm botanicals gathered from elevated Himalayan landscapes.
Every cup is designed to quiet the mind, soften the pace of the day, and return warmth back into the body.
